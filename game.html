Game Instructions

Here are the instructions for the game:

  1. Step 1: The Setup Stage
    Enter the number of dice (between 3 and 6)

  2. Step 2: The play Stage
    Click on Roll Dice and Let's Roll!

  3. Step 3: The End Stage
    Shows final results (rounds played, points ,average points of rounds ).

    4. Here is how the points works:

    • When all of the dice have the same value, the points earned are 60 plus the total value of all the dice.
    • If all but one of the dice have the same value, the points earned are 40 plus the total value of all the dice.
    • When the dice form a sequence (such as 1-2-3 or 4-5-6), the points earned are 20 plus the total value of all the dice.
    • If the dice do not form a sequence and do not all have the same value, the points earned are equal to the sum of the values of all the dice.
    • Any other outcome results in zero points being earned.
