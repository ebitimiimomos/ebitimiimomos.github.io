In this project, I created a simple personal website using HTML and CSS. The website contains different sections to showcase various aspects of myself, including personal information, career goals, educational history, work experience, skills, hobbies, interests, and achievements.

The website's structure is designed with a header that includes navigation buttons to quickly access different sections. It starts with a section displaying my personal information, including a picture and a brief introduction. I introduce myself as a Business and Data Enthusiast with a passion for food and media. I mention my family background and my pursuit of higher education in the UK.

Next, I elaborate on my career goals, emphasizing my creative nature and interest in using business and media to make a positive impact on society. I express my long-term ambition of becoming a successful businesswoman and outline the roles I see myself excelling in over the next five years.

The website also includes a section that lists my educational history, highlighting my MSc in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from the University of Liverpool and my BA(Hons) in Business Management and Economics from De Montfort University.

The Work Experience section describes my various roles, such as Team Leader - Engagement Associate and Racial Advisor at De Montfort University, International Student Officer at De Montfort University Student's Union, and Teaching and Learning Enhancement Pedagogy Research Member at De Montfort University. I provide a brief summary of my responsibilities and contributions in each role.

The Skills section showcases my soft skills, hard skills, and additional skills. I use icons to represent each skill, including communication, coding/programming, teamwork/collaboration, data analysis, social media marketing, leadership, project management, and more.

I also dedicate a section to my Hobbies, Interests, and Achievements. I mention my hobbies such as writing, creative crafts, and playing Ultimate Frisbee. My interests include coding/programming, content creation, and business. I also list my achievements, such as being awarded First Class in my BA(Hons), winning awards for excellence at De Montfort University, and being voted as the Best Performing Activity Leader at Alpadia International.
